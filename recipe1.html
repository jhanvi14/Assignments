Recipes-World's Best Scrambled Eggs

I adopted this recipe from a book called Sydney Food by Bill Granger. Ever since tasting these eggs on my 1st visit to Bill's Restaurant in King's Cross,Sydney. I have been after the recipe. I have since transformed it into what I really believe are the best scrambled eggs I have ever tasted.

This recipe is what I call a "Very special breakfast" , just look at the ingredients to see why.It has to be tasted to be believed.

Ingredients

The following ingredients make one serving:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of butter(10g)
  • 1/3 cup of cream
  • A pinch of salt
  • Freshly miled black pepper
  • 2 fresh chives(chopped)

Instruction

  1. Whisk eggs,cream, and salt in a bowl
  2. Melt the butter in a non stick pan over a high heat(taking care not to heat the butter)
  3. Pour egg mixture into pan and wait untilit starts setting around the edge of tha pan(around 20 seconds)
  4. Using a wooden spatula,bring the mixture into the center as if it were an omelet, and let it cook for another 20 seconds
  5. Fold contents in again,leave for 20 seconds,and repeat it until the eggs are only just done
  6. Grind a light sprinkling pf freshly miled pepper over the eggs and blend in some chopped fresh chives.

You should only make a maximum of two servings per fryig pan.
